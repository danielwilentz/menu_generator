About the Project


This project is primarily a means for me to explore the work flow and techniques associated with NLP (natural language processing) in an unsupervised setting. To create the Cuisine Classifier, I grabbed titles from 48,000 posts on the subreddit r/foodporn corresponding to the top 1000 posts per month for the last 4 years (48 months). After obtaining my data, I employed various methods to clean it (elementary methods such as making all text lowercase/removing punctuation, and more advanced methods such as filtering advertisements and non-english posts). Once I cleaned the text, I tried a variety of vectorization/dimensionality-reduction/clustering techniques. My best solution involved using a TF-IDF vectorizer coupled with Non-Negative Matrix Factorization.
